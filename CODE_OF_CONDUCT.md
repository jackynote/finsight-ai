# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making this project a respectful, inclusive, and professional space for contributors and users.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct and constructive.
- Critique code, not people.
- Keep discussion focused on the technical work.
- Respect privacy and sensitive financial data.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, intimidation, or abusive language
- Deliberate disclosure of personal or financial data
- Spam, trolling, or disruption of reviews and issue threads

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or reject contributions that violate this code of conduct.
